About

The person behind the simulations

I'm a PhD physicist (Jožef Stefan Institute, Ljubljana) with a career built around electromagnetic and thermal simulation. Before going independent, I worked as a senior R&D engineer at GEM motors and currently hold a Lead Development Engineer role at Iskraemeco, a smart metering company in Slovenia.

My core toolset is Ansys Electronics Desktop — Maxwell, HFSS, Icepak, SIwave — complemented by Python and PyAEDT for parametric automation and design optimization. I take on freelance projects where precision and physical rigor matter.
